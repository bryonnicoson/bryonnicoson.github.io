US State Trivia

Test your knowledge of the shapes of the fifty US states! App randomly presents the fifty images along with 1 correct answer button and 3 unique distractor buttons and keeps a running score. My nephew Aidan got 49/50 the first time he tried it. Kids these days.

This was my first Android project for ADI. I intend to go back and update it and add some other features. And beat my nephew's score.

WishBone Canine Rescue

WishBone Canine Rescue is a foster-based not-for-profit dog rescue in Bloomington-Normal, IL. They are a pet project of mine (pun intended). Seriously, though, I love dogs, and I love helping this cause. The app is currently in early alpha on the Google Play Store. It retrieves Petfinder data via a passthrough Heroku ruby server, which parses XML to JSON and handles some data formatting. I'd love the opportunity to explain to you why I'm handling the API call this way.

Shake It Up!

This was our ADI team project. We learned a lot about Git workflows and team development. This app uses satellite and wifi to get your current latitude and longitude, retrieves your stored search preferences from a local SQLite database, and then passes these parameters to a Yelp API query, returning a sorted list of nearby eateries matching your filter settings. I handled the Restaurant model, Yelp call, and am particularly proud of the Filter Activity.
